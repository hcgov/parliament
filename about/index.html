About

The Parliament of the Democratic Republic of Hack Club is the main Legislative body within the nation. The purpose of the Parliament is to create, amend, and vote on bills to represent the citizens of the Democratic Republic of Hack Club.

The Parliament is composed of Members of Parliament (MPs), which are elected by citizens of the Nation in General Elections



The Parliament of Hack Club (Hack Club Parliament) and other associated communities and entities are not associated with the Hack Club non-profit organization. This is an unofficial community group and only exists for the purpose of entertainment.
