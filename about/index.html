About

The Parliament of the Democratic Republic of Hack Club is the main Legislative body within the nation. The purpose of the Parliament is to create, amend, and vote on bills to represent the citizens of the Democratic Republic of Hack Club.

The Parliament is composed of Members of Parliament (MPs), which are elected by citizens of the Nation in General Elections every two to three months. Parties are given seats based on how many votes they get in the General Election, and each MP gets allocated seats from their party.

If you have any inquiries, or would like to participate, join the Slack channel.
