It is the duty of the citizens of the United Republic of Hack Club to partake in the general elections to elect their Parliamentary Assembly, Prime Minister, and Lead National Attorney. All hack clubbers are eligible URHC citizens and can vote virtually or in-person at designated polling locations.


The Parliamentary Assembly

is an assembly of elected officials that can hold hearings, hold special votes, and most importantly draft laws/legislation


The Prime Minister

is lead representative in the Parliamentary Assembly, and oversees additional government agencies along with holding greater voting power in the assembly.


The Lead National Attorney

is in charge of the HCNAO (Hack Club National Attorney's Office). The HCNAO handles criminal prosecutions (not civil) and class-action lawsuits, alongside representing the government in the defense of passed and legally charged legislations.
